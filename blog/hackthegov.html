Tech&Meet: Hack The Holidays

On December 17, 2024, I attended a Tech&Meet session with Robbe Verwilghen, a Howest alumnus and ethical hacker.

He discussed his experience in Hack The Government, a Belgian competition allowing ethical hackers to assess government infrastructure.

Robbe explained his path from bug bounty hunting to becoming a penetration tester and QA engineer, including his work with Intigriti.

Despite some content being classified, it was a highly motivational session showing how passion can lead to a strong cybersecurity career.
